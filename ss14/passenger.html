Okay, you've arrived at SS14, and you're ready to start playing the game.

Uh, how DO you play?


When you join the game, you'll be in the lobby. There'll probably be a round on-going, so you'll need to late-join.


Lobby? Round? What?

Game time on SS14 is typically cut up into rounds. It typically takes ~1-2 hours for one to end, and in that time you're not allowed to talk about it anywhere else, since that's called Metacomming (or, Metacommunications)

Late-joining is when you join the game while a round is on-going. Simple, really.


How do I late join?

Press the Join button in the lobby. Then, select the job you want to play.

Since this is a guide about the Passenger role, pick Passenger.

I'm in game, now what?

Learn. Talk to people, whatever.

There'll be some random things going on, but don't worry. You'll be fine. Probably.

Some things you need to know:

  Again, Follow the rules.

  The round only ends when the round-end screen appears. Not before.

  You're probably going to die. Don't worry, this happens to the best of us.

  Don't try to get into places you're not allowed inside. This is called greytiding (or graytiding), and is not okay in the slightest.


I died, now what?

Now you wait. And wait.

Know that you have a chance to be put back into the round. Do not use any info you got while you're a ghost in-game.

Also, don't beg for the round to restart. It's not against the rules or anything, but your complaints will fall on deaf ears, and other people won't care.
