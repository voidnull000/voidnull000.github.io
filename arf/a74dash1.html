ATTENTION
THIS ANOMALY IS CURRENTLY NOT CONTAINED.
PRELIMINARY TESTING SHOWS THAT THIS ANOMALY IS GENERALLY HARMLESS. HOWEVER, CAUTION IS STILL ADVISED.
IF YOU OR SOMEONE YOU KNOW IS HOLDING THE ANOMALY, OR INFORMATION ABOUT ITS WEREABOUTS, CONTACT US IMMEDIATELY.


Details
Anomaly 47-1 (classified as A74-1) is a humanoid organism which appears to possess rational thought. It is a skeletal figure, with strange fleshy tendrils wrapped around itself, and its internal organs are exposed.
A74-1 appears to be sentient, and capable of emotion. It, however, cannot speak. Why this is remains inconclusive, but general tests are underway to understand this issue further.
An interesting feature of A74-1 is the color and pattern on its eyeballs. The sclera is bright yellow, whilst the pupil appears to be a deep red. There also appears to be, what can only be described as, triangle shapes above and below the pupil, potentially being its iris.

Origins
A74-1 was "grown" from A74, emerging from the fleshy surface like a plant. After a careful cleaning and trimming process by the research team, A74-1 was subject to immediate capability and sapience tests.

Touring information
A74-1 is unavailable for tour, due to its recent existence and cataloguing.
