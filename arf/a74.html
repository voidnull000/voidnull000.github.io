Anomaly 47


Details
Anomaly 47 (classified as A74) is a large organism of considerable size. It consists of a large organic surface with a sizeable trunk protuding from it. The trunk looks similar to the cardiovascular system of a human being, complete with a contained heart. The "branches" of the organism are made of bone, and the "leaves" are a massive interconnected mass of lungs.
Tests have determined that the organism extends to at least five meters downward from the visible surface. Whether this mass contains the internal organs is yet to be determined.
Whilst the ARF has not been able to determine if it is capable of rational thought, it has been demonstrated to have a brain, and potentially an intelligence of some kind. Further studies are ongoing in order to conclude these questions.
Despite general anxiety between numerous researchers, the ARF has determined that A74 is safe. While A74 can consume organic material (i.e. bones, organs, skin, dead flesh), it has been demonstrated to not consume any living being, especially while wearing clothing.

Origins
Anomaly 47 was created by notable scientist Nia Laborde, in what they claim was another attempt at making a new species. When asked on details, they claimed that A74 was attempt 12, and was abandoned due to numerous errors in the creation process.
Said scientist is frequently invited to the ARF facility for questioning and help on feeding. If you see them on your tour, feel free to say hello.

Touring information
Anomaly 47 is available for viewing on tour only during dedicated events (i.e. "Deep Access Week"). Please refer to the tour calender for details and booking dates. Tours consist of a viewing from the 3rd story observation window.
Access inside of the containment chamber is strictly permitted only to research personnel, due to the risk of contamination, sanitary restrictions, and general disease risk.
